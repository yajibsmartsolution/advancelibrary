About Our eLibrary

Our History

Established in 1989, the Sa'adatu Rimi College of Education Library has grown from a modest collection of print materials to a state-of-the-art digital library serving thousands of students and faculty members.

In 2015, we launched our digital transformation initiative, which has resulted in the development of this comprehensive eLibrary platform that provides 24/7 access to our growing collection of digital resources.

Our Collections

Our eLibrary boasts an extensive collection of over 50,000 ebooks, 15,000 academic journals, 5,000 theses and dissertations, and numerous multimedia resources across all disciplines offered at the college.

Services Offered

  • 24/7 access to digital resources
  • Research assistance and reference services
  • Information literacy training
  • Document delivery services
  • Institutional repository management

Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) in English

Program Overview

The B.Ed. in English program prepares students to become competent English language teachers at secondary school level. The program combines linguistic theory with practical teaching methodology.

Duration

4 Years (8 Semesters)

Entry Requirements

Five O'Level credits including English and Literature in English, plus acceptable UTME score.
